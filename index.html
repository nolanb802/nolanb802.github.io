About Us

Mama Mia's is a family-run Italian restaurant located in Jericho, Vermont. We pride ourselves with serving outstanding hand-tossed, gourmet pizza and homemade Italian dishes along with exceptional service. Our pasta is hand made on-site, and our Italian food is made with the finest ingredients, bringing old world flair to our family style restaurant. Our menu features gourmet brick oven pizza, Italian specialty dishes such as veal, seafood, chicken, and pasta. All pizza and dishes are made to order. The owner, Mama Mia, oversees that all dishes are prepped and served with love, and that everyone who dines at Mama Mia's feels like it's their home as well.
